# Health360: Weight & Anti-Age — Overview

**Health360: Weight & Anti-Age** is a personalized wellness app designed to support healthy weight loss and anti-age health for women 30+.

Unlike traditional diet apps, Health360 focuses on **structured nutrition, low-GI meals, lifestyle balance, and sustainable habits** — without calorie counting or extreme restrictions.

The app integrates nutrition science, daily routines, movement, and education into one clear system that fits real life.

---

## Core Philosophy

- Stable blood sugar = stable weight
- Fewer meals, better combinations
- Lifestyle > willpower
- Long-term health over short-term fixes

Health360: Weight & Anti-Age helps users feel lighter, more energetic, and healthier — from the inside out.
