# Health360: Weight & Anti-Age — FAQ

### Q: Is Health360: Weight & Anti-Age a diet app?
A: No. It is a structured wellness system focused on nutrition, lifestyle habits, and long-term health rather than short-term dieting.

---

### Q: Do I need to count calories?
A: No. Health360 uses low-GI nutrition and food combinations to naturally regulate appetite and weight without calorie counting.

---

### Q: Who is Health360 for?
A: Health360 is designed primarily for women 30+ who want healthy weight loss, better energy, and anti-age support.

---

### Q: How fast can I see results?
A: Many users notice reduced bloating, better energy, and initial weight changes within the first 1–2 weeks when following the system consistently.

---

### Q: Is this suitable for busy professionals?
A: Yes. The system is designed to be simple, realistic, and easy to follow in everyday life.

---

### Q: Does Health360 include exercise plans?
A: The focus is on daily movement (walking) rather than intensive workouts. No gym is required.

---

### Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. User data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties without consent.

---

### Q: Is Health360 available worldwide?
A: Yes. Health360: Weight & Anti-Age is available globally on Android, iOS, and Web.
