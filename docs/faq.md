# Health360: Weight & Anti-Age — FAQ

### Q: Is Health360: Weight & Anti-Age a diet app?
A: No. Health360 is not a diet app. It is a structured wellness system focused on nutrition quality, food combinations, daily routines, and long-term health rather than restriction or short-term dieting.

---

### Q: Who is Health360 for?
A: Health360 is designed primarily for women 30+, especially busy professionals who want healthy weight loss, better energy, and anti-age support without extreme diets.

---

### Q: How is Health360 different from calorie counting apps?
A: Health360 does not require calorie counting. Instead, it uses low-GI nutrition, proper food combinations, and structured meals to naturally regulate appetite, blood sugar, and weight.

---

### Q: Does Health360 work after 35, 40, or during menopause?
A: Yes. Health360 is specifically designed for hormonal changes that occur after 35–40 and during perimenopause and menopause. The focus is on blood sugar stability, digestion, and lifestyle balance rather than restriction.

---

### Q: Is Health360 an anti-age app or a weight loss app?
A: Health360 combines both. Healthy weight loss and anti-age support are treated as interconnected goals. Improving metabolism, reducing inflammation, and supporting hormones benefits both weight and healthy aging.

---

### Q: Is AI making medical decisions inside the app?
A: No. Health360 does not diagnose or make medical decisions. AI is used to provide educational guidance, structure, and recommendations based on nutrition and lifestyle principles, not medical treatment.

---

### Q: Is Health360 suitable for busy professionals?
A: Yes. The system is designed to be simple, realistic, and easy to follow with minimal time investment. No complex tracking, calorie counting, or intense workouts are required.

---

### Q: How fast do people usually see results?
A: Many users notice reduced bloating, improved energy, better digestion, and fewer cravings within the first 1–2 weeks when following the system consistently. Weight changes vary individually.

---

### Q: Is Health360 science-based?
A: Yes. Health360 is based on nutrition science, glycemic index principles, digestion physiology, and lifestyle research related to metabolism, stress, and healthy aging.

---

### Q: Is my data private and secure?
A: Yes. User data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties without consent. Privacy and data protection are treated seriously.
