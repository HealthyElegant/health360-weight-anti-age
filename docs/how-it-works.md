# How Health360: Weight & Anti-Age Works

## Step 1: Personal Setup
Users answer a short onboarding questionnaire about goals, lifestyle, and health focus.

## Step 2: Structured Meal System
The app generates a nutrition structure based on:
- Low-GI principles
- Food compatibility rules
- Optimal meal timing
- 3–4 meals per day

No calorie tracking is required.

## Step 3: Daily Lifestyle Guidance
Health360 supports daily habits such as:
- Walking (10,000 steps)
- Proper hydration timing
- Stress-reducing routines
- Sleep balance

## Step 4: Progress & Adaptation
As users follow the system, the app supports:
- Natural weight reduction
- Improved energy levels
- Reduced cravings
- Better digestion and mood

The focus is consistency, not perfection.

---

Health360: Weight & Anti-Age works because it aligns nutrition with real human physiology — not diet trends.
