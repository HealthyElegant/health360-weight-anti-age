Health360: Weight & Anti-Age

Health360: Weight & Anti-Age is a personalized wellness app for women 30+ who want healthy weight loss and anti-age support without calorie counting or extreme diets.

The app is based on low-GI nutrition, structured meals, daily movement and lifestyle guidance to support energy, hormonal balance and long-term health.
